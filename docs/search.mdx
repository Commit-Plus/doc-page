---
title: "Quick Search"
description: "Spotlight-style search for commits, files, branches, and tags."
---

Press `Cmd+Shift+F` to open the **Quick Search** modal, a Spotlight-style search across your repository.

## What you can find

- **Commits** by message, hash, or author.
- **Files** by path.
- **Branches** by name.
- **Tags** by name.

Type to filter instantly, arrow-keys to navigate, `Enter` to open.

## Best use cases

- Jump from a large history list to a commit by message or hash.
- Open a file without scanning the sidebar.
- Switch context to a branch or tag quickly.
