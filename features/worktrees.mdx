# Smarter Worktree Management

Manage **git worktrees** visually from the sidebar for isolated parallel feature work. Keep your main checkout untouched while you work on something else.

## Available now

- **List** worktrees with branch, path, lock, and dirty-state context.
- **Open** a worktree in a new Commit+ window or in Terminal.
- **Create** and **remove** worktrees from the sidebar.
- **Lock**, **unlock**, **prune**, and **move** worktrees.
- **Check out** branches inside a worktree.

## Why it matters

Worktrees make parallel feature work safer. You can keep one checkout on the main branch, open another checkout for a risky change, and move between them without stashing or repeatedly switching the same working directory.
